## Part 2: Submit to Google Search Console (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Google Search Console
1. Open a new browser tab
2. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
3. Sign in with your Google account (create one if needed)

### Step 2: Add Your Property
1. Click **"Add Property"** (usually a button in the top left)
2. Select **"URL prefix"** (not "Domain")
3. Enter your full URL: `https://thelivewell.manus.space`
4. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 3: Verify Ownership
Google will ask you to verify that you own the domain. Choose one method:

**Method A: HTML File (Easiest)**
1. Google gives you a file name (like `google1234567890.html`)
2. Download this file
3. Go to your Livewell admin dashboard
4. Go to **"Files"** section
5. Upload the file
6. Return to Google Search Console
7. Click **"Verify"**

**Method B: HTML Tag (Also Easy)**
1. Google gives you an HTML tag to add
2. Go to your Livewell admin dashboard
3. Go to **"Settings"** → **"General"**
4. Find a field for "Verification" or "Meta Tag"
5. Paste the HTML tag
6. Click **"Save"**
7. Return to Google Search Console
8. Click **"Verify"**

**Method C: DNS Record (Advanced)**
- Skip this unless you're comfortable with DNS settings

### Step 4: Submit Your Sitemap
Once verified:
1. In Google Search Console, look for **"Sitemaps"** in the left menu
2. Click **"Sitemaps"**
3. In the text box, enter: `sitemap.xml`
4. Click **"Submit"**
5. You should see a success message

### Step 5: Check Indexing Status
1. Go to **"Coverage"** in the left menu
2. You should see your pages listed
3. Google will start crawling and indexing your site

## Part 5: Monitor Your Performance

### Check Google Search Console Weekly
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click your property
3. Look at:
   - **Performance:** See search queries, clicks, impressions
   - **Coverage:** Check for indexing errors
   - **Enhancements:** See rich results and structured data

### Key Metrics to Track
| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Impressions** | How many times your site appeared in search results |
| **Clicks** | How many people clicked to visit your site |
| **CTR** | Click-through rate (clicks ÷ impressions) |
| **Average Position** | Where you rank (1 = first result) |

### Target Metrics
- **Month 1:** 10-50 impressions
- **Month 3:** 100-500 impressions
- **Month 6:** 500-2,000 impressions
- **Month 12:** 2,000-5,000+ impressions

## Troubleshooting

### "Publish button is grayed out"
**Solution:** Create a checkpoint first (see Part 1, Step 2)

### "Verification failed"
**Solution:** 
- Make sure you're using the correct URL format
- Wait 24 hours and try again
- Try a different verification method

### "Sitemap not found"
**Solution:**
- Make sure you entered `sitemap.xml` (not the full URL)
- Check that your site is live and accessible
- Wait 24 hours and try again

### "Pages not indexed after 1 week"
**Solution:**
- Check Coverage tab in Search Console for errors
- Make sure your robots.txt allows indexing
- Submit individual URLs for faster indexing
