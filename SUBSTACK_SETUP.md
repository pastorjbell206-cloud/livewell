## Troubleshooting

**Articles not syncing?**
- Verify the feed URL is correct (test it in your browser)
- Make sure articles are published (not draft) on Substack
- Try manual sync first: click **Sync Substack** button
- Wait 10-30 seconds for sync to complete

**Duplicate articles?**
- Articles are deduplicated by URL
- If you republish on Substack, it won't create duplicates

**Want to stop syncing?**
- Go to **Content Sync** and clear the Substack URL field
- Click **Save**
- Manual articles will remain on your site
