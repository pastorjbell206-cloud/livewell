# How to Find and Click the Publish Button

## The Publish Button Location

The Publish button is in the **Manus Management UI**, which is a separate panel from your website. Here's how to find it:

### Step 1: Look for the Management UI Icon
On your Livewell website, look at the **top right corner** of the page. You should see:
- A **hamburger menu icon** (three horizontal lines) OR
- A **settings/gear icon** OR  
- A **panel icon** (looks like a sidebar)

### Step 2: Click the Icon
Click on that icon in the top right corner. This will open the **Management UI panel** on the right side of your screen.

### Step 3: Find the Publish Button
Once the Management UI panel opens, you should see:
- **Dashboard** tab
- **Code** tab
- **Database** tab
- **Settings** tab
- And at the **top right** of this panel, you'll see the **"Publish"** button (usually blue/highlighted)

### What the Publish Button Looks Like
- **Color:** Blue or highlighted
- **Text:** "Publish" or "Deploy"
- **Location:** Top right corner of the Management UI panel

## Common Issues

### "I see the Management UI but no Publish button"
**Solution:** 
1. Look at the very top of the Management UI panel
2. Scroll up if needed
3. The Publish button should be in the header area

### "The Publish button is grayed out"
**Solution:** 
1. You need to save a checkpoint first
2. Look for a "Save" or "Checkpoint" button
3. Click it to save your changes
4. Then the Publish button will become active (blue/clickable)

### "I don't see a Management UI panel at all"
**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're on your Livewell website (not somewhere else)
2. Look in the top right corner more carefully
3. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
4. Try a different browser

## The Publish Process

Once you find and click the Publish button:

1. **Click "Publish"** → A dialog appears
2. **Choose your domain** → Select `thelivewell.manus.space`
3. **Click "Confirm"** → Deployment starts
4. **Wait 30-60 seconds** → Your site goes live!
5. **You'll see a confirmation** → Your site is now published!
