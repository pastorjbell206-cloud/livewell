## Troubleshooting

**Q: I see a blank page**
- Wait 5 seconds for the page to load
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear your browser cache

**Q: I see "Access Denied"**
- You may not be logged in
- Click "Sign Out" then "Sign In" to log back in
- Make sure you're using the correct Manus account

**Q: I can't find the admin dashboard**
- Use the direct URL: `https://3000-ileby4dcwxdgxh2mvz5vv-d1a8ab42.us1.manus.computer/admin`
- Don't try to find it through menus - just go directly to this URL

**Q: The page loads but nothing appears**
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Clear your browser cookies
